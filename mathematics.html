Mathematical Foundation

Definition: Information Processing System (IPS)

An entity qualifies as an IPS if it:

  • Takes binary information as input
  • Pays time and energy to process information
  • Maximizes goals while minimizing costs (negotiates with reality)
  • Generates outputs that affect future states
